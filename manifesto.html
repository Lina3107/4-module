THE PROCESS
BEHIND DETAG

Brand creates clothing from materials that already exist. The process begins with the search for discarded,
unused, or forgotten textiles; leftover fabrics, old garments, damaged pieces, and materials that would otherwise
lose their value. Each textile is carefully selected, cleaned, studied, and sorted by texture, color, condition,
and potential use. Instead of hiding the past of the material, Detag keeps small traces of it visible, seams,
marks, fading, irregular edges, and signs of wear become part of the final design.
